CARBON


PROPERTIES
1)It is the first element of the group. it is the only non-metal in the group. (C).
2)it does not react with acids/water & other materials but it reacts with OXYGEN
3)It burns in air forming CARBON DIOXIDE {CO2}
4)Carbon has crystalline structures: Diamond & Graphite


Reaction of carbon with oxygen
C+O2-->CO2

Reaction of carbon with calcium oxide

3C+CaO-->CaC2+CO

Reaction of carbon with sulphur
C+2S-->CS2
