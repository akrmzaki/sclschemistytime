BARIUM


PROPERTIES
1)Barium is a silvery-white metal that can be found in the environment
2)Barium is not an extensively used element.
3)All barium compounds are toxic
4)Barium compounds are used in pyrotechnics, petroleum production, and radiology.
5)Pure barium is a pale yellow, somehow shiny.
6)The atomic number of barium is 56.
7)the melting point of barium is 725 °C.
8)the boiling point of barium is 1640 °C.

Reaction with air
Ba(s) + O2(g) --> BaO2(s)
Reaction with acids
Ba(s) + 2 HCl(aq) BaCl2(aq) + H2(g).
Reaction with HYDROGEN
Ba(s) + H2(g) --> BaH2(s)
