INDIUM

Properties
1)It is considered a metal
2) Its atomic number is 49.
3)Its electronegativity is 1.78
4)Its density is 7.31
5) melting point is 156.6 Celsius
6)Its boiling point is 2072 Celsius
7)Indium is a soft, silvery metal.
8) The name of the element is derived from the Latin word for indigo, indicum, because of the bright violet line of its atomic spectrum.
9) It is found in the Earth’s crust at a concentration of 0.1 ppm, making it the 69th most abundant element.
10) It is found in the rare mineral indite [FeIn2S4], and in trace emounts in some zinc ores; it has also been found as the free metal.
11)Indium is used in alloys with other metals to lower their melting points, and is also used in some dental appliances.
12)It’s electronic configuration is Kr 4d10 5s2 5p1
