BERYLLIUM


PROPERTIES
1)Beryllium is a silvery-white, lustrous, relatively soft metal
2)The metal is unaffected by air or water, even at red heat
3)When copper and nickel are alloyed with beryllium they not only become much better at conducting electricity, but they display remarkable elasticity
4)Beryllium and its compounds are toxic and carcinogenic
5)If beryllium dust or fumes are inhaled, it can lead to an incurable inflammation of the lungs called berylliosis.
6)Beryllium is found in about 30 different mineral species.
7)The metal is usually prepared by reducing beryllium fluoride with magnesium metal.
8)Beryllium is used in gears and cogs particularly in the aviation industry

Reaction with air
2 Be(s) + O2(g) ---> 2 BeO(s)
Reaction with acids
Ba(s) + 2 H+(aq) ---> Ba2+(aq) + H2(g)
Reaction with halogens

Be(s) + Cl2(g) --> BeCl2(s)
Be(s) + Br2(g) --> BeBr2(s)
Reaction with water
beryllium not react with water
